About Planetside

Planetside 2 is the sequel to Planetside, released on May 20, 2003. This game is a big MMOFPS, which Planetside 2 has taken to an even greater level. The Planetside team wanted to give their players an epic scale combat situation, and created a huge map able to support several players at the same time. The point of the game is quite simple, capture the other empires bases. The more bases your faction has, the more resources it will generate for your disposal. In the game you can choose between 3 different empires:

Terran Republic (TR)
New Conglomerate (NC)
Vanu Sovereignty (VS)

Each of these empires are fighting for world dominance.

The interresting part of Planetside must be the fact that there is no goal. Or to rephrace that, the goal in unachievable! 3 empires fighting for world dominance makes it impossible to capture every base on the map, before one of the other empires has taken another base back. Also, every faction has an uncapturable warpgate on all 4 different regions. This assures that a faction never gets terminated by the other empires and so the game goes on.

2 objectives, lots of bases. Assault an enemy base, or defend a friendly one. Regardless of what you choose, you will find yourself in a place swarming with enemies. Help your teammates by beeing a combat medic, or delivier heavy damage as an infiltrator with a sniper rifle. Tanks, airships, quadbikes, all this and much more in a war that never ends.
